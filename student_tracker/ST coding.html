About Student Tracker

Learn more about our application and team

Our Mission

Student Tracker is designed to help students manage their academic workload effectively. Our mission is to reduce stress and improve productivity by providing a simple, intuitive platform for tracking assignments, deadlines, and academic progress.

Features

  • Task and assignment management with priority levels
  • Calendar view for visualizing deadlines
  • Progress tracking and statistics
  • Reminder notifications for upcoming deadlines
  • User-friendly interface designed specifically for students
